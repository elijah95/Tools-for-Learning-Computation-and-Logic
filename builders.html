Multiset

Sometimes we speak of a multiset or a comprehension, which is a set whose elements are allowed to have a multiplicity greater than 1. The expression $\{x \mid x \in S \land P(x)\}$ is interpreted as: "the set of elements $x$ in $S$ such that $x$ has property $P$." and is formally refered to as a $\it set$ $\it former$.
